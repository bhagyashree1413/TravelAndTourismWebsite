How to Pack Like a Pro for a 2-Week Trip

Back to Blog

By Team Wanderlust | Oct 15, 2024

Pack Smart, Travel Better

Packing for a two-week trip can be a challenge. How do you fit everything you need without overpacking? The key is to pack smart. Start with a versatile wardrobe based on a neutral color palette, allowing you to mix and match outfits easily. Rolling your clothes instead of folding them can save a surprising amount of space.

One of our top tips is to use packing cubes to organize your belongings. This not only keeps your suitcase tidy but also makes it easy to find what you need without unpacking everything. Remember to pack a universal travel adapter, a portable power bank, and a basic first-aid kit. With these tips, you'll be able to pack efficiently and enjoy a stress-free trip, knowing you have everything you need.

A Food Lover's Guide to Italy

Back to Blog

By Team Wanderlust | Oct 02, 2024

A Culinary Journey

Traveling to Italy is as much a culinary journey as it is a cultural one. Italian cuisine is incredibly diverse, with each region boasting its own unique specialties. In Rome, you can't miss Cacio e Pepe, a simple yet delicious pasta dish. Head to Naples, the birthplace of pizza, for an authentic Neapolitan slice.

Further north in Bologna, you'll find rich and savory dishes like Tagliatelle al Ragù. And of course, no trip to Italy is complete without indulging in gelato. Our advice is to seek out small, family-run trattorias and osterias for the most authentic and memorable meals. Don't be afraid to ask for local recommendations—Italians are passionate about their food and always happy to share their favorite spots.
